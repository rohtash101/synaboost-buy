SynaBoost Ingredients

SynaBoost is formulated with a carefully selected blend of plant-based ingredients, many of which are inspired by traditional Amazonian remedies for supporting brain health. Each ingredient has been chosen for its unique ability to boost cognitive function, mental clarity, and focus. Below are the active ingredients in SynaBoost and their roles in promoting brain health:

Here's a closer look at the key ingredients that make up SynaBoost:

  1. Caffeine from Green Coffee Bean :  Caffeine is the key ingredient in SynaBoost, sourced from green coffee bean extract. It is one of the most popular supplements for brain health. Research shows that caffeine can enhance both physical and mental energy, and some studies link it to improved test scores. SynaBoost contains a moderate amount of caffeine, much less than a large cup of coffee, which has about 150mg. The caffeine is derived from unroasted coffee beans.
  2. Tyrosine :  L-tyrosine, an amino acid, is another important ingredient in SynaBoost. Many people take L-tyrosine for brain health and energy. In the brain, it helps produce neurotransmitters that send signals throughout the body. According to Mount Sinai, L-tyrosine is particularly linked to neurotransmitters like epinephrine, norepinephrine, and dopamine.
  3. Theacrine :  Theacrine works similarly to caffeine by influencing the central nervous system to enhance cognition. It is a natural compound found in tea. Research has shown that tea consumption is associated with better cognitive health, possibly due to theacrine. A specific form of theacrine, called Teacrine, has been developed to concentrate its effects. A study in 2015 found that theacrine improved cognitive performance and mood in young adults.
  4. Theobromine :  Theobromine is a naturally occurring antioxidant found in chocolate. Many individuals consume chocolate daily for its health benefits, wellness properties, and anti-inflammatory effects. It is often believed that the beneficial effects of chocolate are primarily attributed to the theobromine it contains. Numerous studies have linked theobromine to notable improvements in cognitive health. For instance, a study conducted in 2017 demonstrated that theobromine enhanced the levels of brain-derived neurotrophic factor (BDNF) and facilitated learning processes. However, it is important to note that this research was conducted on mice rather than humans.
  5. Bacopa Monnieri :  Bacopa monnieri is widely recognized as a prominent plant in the realm of cognitive enhancement supplements. Many individuals utilize bacopa monnieri independently to promote brain health. The bacopa monnieri included in SynaBoost is standardized to ensure it contains 20% of its most potent component: bacosides. A neuropharmacological review of bacopa monnieri revealed that the extract improved cerebral blood flow, exhibited antioxidant properties, and reduced the formation of beta-amyloid plaques.
  6. Huperzia Serrata :  SynaBoost incorporates Huperzia serrata, another well-regarded plant extract, to enhance focus and cognitive function. The Huperzia serrata in SynaBoost is standardized to contain 1% Huperzine A, the active compound responsible for its cognitive benefits. This plant has a rich history of use in traditional Chinese medicine. A study published in 2021 in the journal Molecules indicated that Huperzine A from Huperzia serrata may assist in addressing Alzheimer’s disease due to its neuroprotective properties, particularly in relation to beta-amyloid plaque formation.
  7. Rhodiola Rosea :  Rhodiola rosea is thought to function as an adaptogen, aiding the body in managing stress. Even in a low-stress environment, individuals are exposed to daily stressors present in their surroundings, including air, water, and food. These stressors can lead to increased inflammation throughout the body, penetrate the blood-brain barrier, and negatively impact both physical and cognitive health.
